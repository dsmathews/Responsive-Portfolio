About Me


My life is amazing! I am a husband, father, teacher, coach, musician, and now a web developer in training. I come from a strong family background with high standards and a strong work ethic.


My background before coming to web development was teaching high school and middle school band for fourteen years. During that time, I was responsible for personnel management, inventory control and acquisiton, finance and budget, as well as communications. These experiences helped me become the person I am today.


I enjoy helping others however I can. My mission in life to is try and spread as much good as possible. I am a member of various local and national organizations. In our community, my family and I attend a local Lutheran church.
